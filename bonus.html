Aubie the Tiger

Aubie the tiger is the mascot of Auburn University Auburn University is a public land-grant research university in Auburn, Alabama

  1. Aubie began as a cartoon character.Aubie's existence began as a cartoon character that first appeared on the Auburn/Hardin-Simmons football program cover on October 3, 1959
  2. Aubie is the official tiger mascot of Auburn University. Aubie is an anthropomorphic tiger.
  3. Aubie, Auburn University's tiger mascot, is in his 37th season as a spirit leader and goodwill ambassador for Auburn University
  4. Aubie has very animated characteristics such as his strut walk, quick turns, and exaggerated pointing

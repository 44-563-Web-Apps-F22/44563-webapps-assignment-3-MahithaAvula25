Favorite Museum: Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)

StarryNight been called Van Gogh's quintessential work. It has been in MoMa's permanent collection since 1941 and was acquired through Lillie P. Bliss Bequest. 'Starry Night' is an oil on canvas and was painted by Van Gogh in a studio on the first floor of the Saint-Paul-de Mausole asylum. The view has been identified as that of his bedroom window and he captured it in the painting at different times of the day: sunrise, moonrise, on sunny, cloudy, windy or rainy days.
